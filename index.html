Field Technician Level Expectations (L1-P5)

Level 1 - Foundation Days 1-14

New hire orientation and basic skills development

  • Complete all HR and compliance paperwork
  • Understand G$D philosophy and Members First culture
  • Basic security system knowledge
  • Navigate CRM and dispatch systems
  • Shadow experienced technicians (min 5 ride-alongs)
  • Pass L1 written assessment (>80%)
Level 2 - Developing Days 15-30

Supervised field work and technical skill building

  • Perform basic installations with supervision
  • Complete standard service calls independently
  • Accurate documentation of all work
  • CC rate <15% on assigned jobs
  • Customer satisfaction >85%
  • Pass L2 practical skills assessment
Level 3 - Competent Days 31-60

Independent field operations and efficiency focus

  • Independent handling of standard installations
  • Troubleshoot common system issues
  • Complete 15-20 jobs per week
  • CC rate <10%, Customer sat >90%
  • NEC code compliance on all work
  • Pass L3 certification exam
Level 4 - Proficient Days 61-90

Full productivity and quality mastery

  • Handle complex installations independently
  • Complete 20-25 jobs per week
  • CC rate <5% (AIP target achieved)
  • Customer satisfaction >95%
  • First-call resolution >85%
  • Pass L4 advanced certification
Pro Level (P1-P5) - Excellence Track 90+ Days

Advanced specialization and leadership development

P1-P2: Specialist
  • 25+ jobs/week consistently
  • Commercial installation capable
  • Training new hires
P3-P4: Expert
  • Territory leadership
  • Quality assurance role
  • Escalation resolution
P5: Master
  • Regional expertise
  • Training program development
  • Leadership pipeline
